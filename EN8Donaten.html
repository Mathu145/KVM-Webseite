Your donation for the care of the cultural association Murugan in Toffen

The Kulurverein Murugan has been founded on 13.04.1992, we have opened at the looslistreet our first and very simple temple through the help of members and public offices. Most of the Hindu temple in Toffen was completed with the support of GHZ architects in Belp, our specialists for sculpture and temple from India and of course with the help of donors, the debt is currently at 2.8 million to the Armotisation deadline to pay are dependent on donations. We are grateful for the various interest-free private loans that we have received. For yet still 300'000 we lack the ongoing costs to ceiling otherwise we run the risk of having to dissolve unique club. The Cultural Association Murugan is committed to the care of the Hindu culture, mainly for the Hindu temple. With us we we value respect and openness. We want the religion and culture more accessible for the population do. People of different ancestry and religions are at any time Welcome. The association is not to achieve profit on Focused. The remaining amount will be used for Charitable.

A special feature of the Cultural Association Murugan is that the board and members of all administrative tasks and work volunteers assume and the small remaining costs fully myself wearing. So donations are used without any deduction to 100% for the Culture Club.

We continue to maintain our culture, we need your support and would be happy if we have to hope for useful dispenser. Every small donation is welcome and gives us hope to continue our volunteer work.
